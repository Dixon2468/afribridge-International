Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need experience to apply for a job abroad?
Not necessarily. We have placements for both skilled and semi-skilled workers. Your chances improve with documentation and readiness to learn.
What countries do you help with?
We assist clients with opportunities in Canada, Qatar, Sweden, Luxembourg, Italy, and Turkey.
How much does a consultation cost?
Our first consultation is completely free. Further services are priced depending on the support you need.
Can you help with student visas?
Yes, we offer complete study abroad guidance, including admission, visa support, and departure prep.
How long does the visa process take?
It varies by country, but typically ranges from 2 weeks to 2 months. We guide you through every stage.
